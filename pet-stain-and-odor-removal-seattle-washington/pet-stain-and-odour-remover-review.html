Does OxiClean remove pet and urine odors?

This pet stain remover, designed for carpets and area rugs, uses a special oxygenation process to effectively remove tough blemishes like vomit, urine, and feces, offering a highly effective solution for odor control.

How long does it take to get dog smell out of a house?

The scent of a dog can persist in a house for several weeks to months, with the duration depending on the odor's intensity and the materials within the home, but employing regular cleaning and utilizing an odor generator can expedite the dissipation of the smell.

Can you mix vinegar and OxiClean?

Avoid mixing OxiClean and vinegar, as OxiClean breaks down into hydrogen peroxide, and combining hydrogen peroxide with vinegar produces peracetic acid, which can be corrosive and cause irritation to the eyes, mouth, nose, and throat.
